# TRAVELER 1: SRI (Indian Passport Holder)

## Visa Type: Visitor Visa (Subclass 600) - Tourist Stream

### Why You Need This Visa
Indian passport holders are **NOT eligible** for the Electronic Travel Authority (ETA). You must apply for the full Visitor Visa through the Australian Department of Home Affairs online system.

### The Good News
- Your **H-1B status and US residence** significantly strengthens your application
- Shows strong ties to a third country (US)
- Demonstrates financial stability and employment
- High approval rate for Indian nationals with valid US work visas

---

## STEP-BY-STEP APPLICATION PROCESS

### STEP 1: Create an ImmiAccount (Week 1)
**Time Required:** 15-20 minutes

1. Go to: **https://online.immi.gov.au/lusc/register**
2. Click "Create ImmiAccount"
3. Fill in personal details:
   - Full name (exactly as on passport)
   - Date of birth
   - Email address (use one you check regularly)
   - Create a strong password
4. Verify your email
5. Set up security questions
6. Log in to confirm account works

**Save these credentials securely - you'll need them to check application status!**

---

### STEP 5: Submit Your Application (Week 2)
**Time Required:** 45-60 minutes

1. Log into ImmiAccount: **https://online.immi.gov.au**
2. Click "New Application"
3. Select: **Visitor Visa (600)** > **Tourist stream** > **Apply outside Australia**
4. Complete all form sections:

**Section 1: Personal Details**
- Enter details EXACTLY as they appear on your passport
- Include all names (given, family, any other names used)

**Section 2: Contact Details**
- US address
- US phone number
- Email address

**Section 3: Passport Details**
- Passport number
- Issue/expiry dates
- Country of issue: India

**Section 4: Travel Details**
- Purpose: Tourism/Holiday
- Intended arrival: February 12, 2026
- Intended departure: February 24, 2026
- Intended duration: 13 days

**Section 5: Employment**
- Current employer details
- Occupation
- Annual income

**Section 6: Character & Health**
- Answer all health questions honestly
- Declare any criminal history (if applicable)

**Section 7: Document Upload**
- Upload all documents prepared in Step 2
- Use clear, legible scans
- File size limits: usually 5MB per document

5. Review entire application
6. Pay the fee: **AUD $190** (credit card)
7. Submit application
8. **SAVE/PRINT the Transaction Reference Number (TRN)**

---

### STEP 6: After Submission

**What Happens Next:**

| Timeline | What to Expect |
|----------|----------------|
| Immediately | Receive email confirmation with TRN |
| 1-7 days | Status changes to "Received" |
| 7-14 days | May receive request for more documents |
| 14-21 days | Status changes to "Assessment in Progress" |
| 20-30 days | Decision made |

**Possible Outcomes:**
1. **Visa Granted** - You'll receive email notification. Visa is electronically linked to passport.
2. **Request for More Information** - Respond within deadline (usually 28 days)
3. **Biometrics Required** - May be asked to visit nearest Australian visa application center

**Check Status:**
- Log into ImmiAccount anytime
- Status updates automatically

---

# TRAVELER 2: GIRLFRIEND (US Passport Holder)

## Visa Type: Electronic Travel Authority (ETA) - Subclass 601

### Why This Is Easy
US passport holders are eligible for the simple ETA system. The entire process takes **5-10 minutes** and approval is usually **instant to 12 hours**.

---

## STEP-BY-STEP APPLICATION PROCESS

### STEP 1: Download the Official ETA App

**iOS (iPhone):**
https://apps.apple.com/app/australian-eta/id1497646223

**Android:**
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=au.gov.homeaffairs.eta

**IMPORTANT:** Only use the official Australian Government app. Third-party websites charge unnecessary fees for the same service.

---

### STEP 2: Prepare Before Opening the App
**Time Required:** 2 minutes

Have ready:
- US Passport (physical passport for NFC scanning)
- Good lighting (for selfie)
- Credit card for $20 AUD payment
- 5 minutes of uninterrupted time

---

### STEP 3: Complete Application in App
**Time Required:** 5-10 minutes

1. **Open App** - Accept terms and conditions

2. **Select Passport Country** - Choose "United States"

3. **Scan Passport**
   - Hold phone near the passport's chip (in back cover)
   - App uses NFC to read chip data
   - This verifies passport authenticity

4. **Take Selfie**
   - App will guide you
   - Look directly at camera
   - Neutral expression
   - Good lighting, no glasses

5. **Answer Declarations**
   - Health questions (any tuberculosis history?)
   - Character questions (any criminal convictions?)
   - Answer honestly - most people answer "No" to everything

6. **Review Information**
   - Verify all details match passport
   - Confirm email address

7. **Pay Fee**
   - AUD $20 (~$13 USD)
   - Credit card or debit card

8. **Submit**

---

### STEP 4: Receive Your ETA

| Outcome | Timeline |
|---------|----------|
| Most Applications | Approved within 12 hours |
| Typical | Approved within minutes |
| Delayed | Up to 72 hours (rare) |

**Notification:**
- Email confirmation sent
- ETA is electronically linked to passport number
- **No physical visa or sticker needed**

---

### STEP 5: Verify ETA Status

**Check Status:**
- VEVO System: https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/already-have-a-visa/check-visa-details-and-conditions/check-conditions-online
- Enter passport details
- Confirms ETA is active

## ETA DETAILS

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Validity** | 12 months from issue |
| **Entries** | Multiple entries allowed |
| **Max Stay per Visit** | 3 months |
| **Purpose** | Tourism, visiting family, business visits |
| **Work Allowed?** | No |
| **Extend in Australia?** | Apply for new visa if needed |

# TROUBLESHOOTING

### Sri: What If Visa Takes Longer?

**If no decision by Jan 20, 2026:**
1. Log into ImmiAccount - check status
2. Call Australia Home Affairs: +61 2 6196 0196
3. Email: eta.online.helpdesk@homeaffairs.gov.au
4. Consider contacting local Australian consulate

**If Request for More Documents:**
- Respond within the deadline (usually 28 days)
- Upload additional documents to ImmiAccount
- Send a polite email confirming submission

### Girlfriend: What If ETA Not Instant?

**If not approved within 24 hours:**
1. Check spam/junk email
2. Log into app - check status
3. Wait 72 hours (maximum processing)
4. Contact: eta.online.helpdesk@homeaffairs.gov.au

**If ETA Rejected (rare):**
- Apply for Visitor Visa 600 instead
- US citizens have high approval rates for both
