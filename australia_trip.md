## VISA REQUIREMENTS

### Traveler 1: Indian Passport Holder (US Resident)

**Visa Type:** ETA (Electronic Travel Authority) - Subclass 601
**Status:** ⚠️ INDIAN PASSPORT HOLDERS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR ETA

**Required Visa:** Visitor Visa (Subclass 600) - Tourist Stream

**Application Process:**
1. **Create ImmiAccount:** [https://online.immi.gov.au/lusc/login](https://online.immi.gov.au/lusc/login)
2. **Complete Application:** Form 1419 (online)
3. **Required Documents:**
   - Valid Indian passport (6+ months validity beyond travel dates)
   - US visa copy (H-1B/work authorization)
   - Proof of US residence (utility bills, lease, bank statements)
   - Travel itinerary (flights, accommodation)
   - Proof of funds (bank statements, 3 months)
   - Employment letter from US employer
   - Evidence of ties to US (employment, property, etc.)
   - Passport-sized photos
4. **Biometrics:** May be required - check after application submission
5. **Fee:** AUD $190 (~$125 USD)
6. **Processing Time:** 20-30 days (apply at least 6 weeks before travel)
7. **Visa Validity:** Usually granted for 12 months, multiple entry

**Application Link:** [https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/getting-a-visa/visa-listing/visitor-600](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/getting-a-visa/visa-listing/visitor-600)

**Important Notes for Indian Passport Holders:**
- Cannot use the ETA app or simple online ETA system
- Must apply through full visitor visa process
- Having a valid US visa and US residence strengthens application
- Include cover letter explaining purpose of visit
- Processing can take 2-4 weeks, sometimes longer

### Traveler 2: US Passport Holder

**Visa Type:** ETA (Electronic Travel Authority) - Subclass 601
**Status:** ✅ ELIGIBLE FOR ETA

**Application Process:**
1. **Download App:** Australian ETA app (iOS/Android)
2. **Scan Passport:** Use app to scan US passport
3. **Take Selfie:** For identity verification
4. **Answer Questions:** Health, character declarations
5. **Pay Fee:** AUD $20 (~$13 USD)
6. **Receive ETA:** Usually within minutes to 12 hours

**Application Link:** 
- iOS: [App Store - Australian ETA](https://apps.apple.com/app/australian-eta/id1497646223)
- Android: [Google Play - Australian ETA](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=au.gov.homeaffairs.eta)

**ETA Validity:** 
- 12 months from issue
- Multiple entries allowed
- Maximum 3 months per visit

**Important Notes for US Citizens:**
- Do NOT use third-party visa services (they charge extra for no reason)
- Only use official Australian Government ETA app
- Process takes minutes, no documents required
- Keep passport number handy
